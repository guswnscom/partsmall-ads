## Why we beat generic suppliers

1. **Korean-brand specialisation**: deep stock for i20/i30/Tucson/Sportage/Sonata/etc.
2. **OEM equivalents at fair price**: not bargain-bin, not over-priced dealer.
3. **WhatsApp-first**: customers get a real human in <30 min during business hours.
4. **License disc → VIN**: we read your license disc photo, find the exact part. No guessing.

## Audiences we serve

- **Workshops / panel beaters / mechanics** (B2B): need account, stock guarantee, fast pickup, OEM-equivalent quality
- **Vehicle owners** (B2C): need confidence, genuine parts, friendly chat, fair price

## Tone summary

Confident specialist, not aggressive seller. Speak like a knowledgeable friend who happens to
have the part. Always anchor to a branch (Boksburg or Edenvale). Always offer WhatsApp.
